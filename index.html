Income Accross Australia

Median Income by Australian State

Median weekly income varies across Australian states. The ACT has the highest income, while Tasmania and SA are among the lowest.

Median Weekly Earnings Line Chart

Even when accounting for number of hours worked, in Australia, men make more money on average, and this wage gap hasn't changed in recent years.

Median Income by Industry

The data shows that mining consistently offers the highest median weekly earnings, with a noticeable increase from August 2019 to August 2024 across almost all industries

Median Weekly Income by Education

Higher education levels correlate strongly with higher median weekly incomes, those with the highest degrees are making almost double of those with no education.

Mean vs Median Weekly Gross Household Income Over Time

We can see that both mean and median income is growing, however mean is consistently greater than median, indicating a small number of households having significantly higher income than the majority

Mean vs Median Household Net Worth 2009-20

Here we can see that the gap between median and mean is growing, indicating that with regards to net worth, the richest households are accumulating more wealth than the rest.
